Blog Data Analysis Introduction

Published: 2026-02-19 | Read time: 12 minutes | Category: Analytics

Data analysis is crucial for blog growth. Understanding your traffic, user behavior, and content performance helps you make data-driven decisions to increase AdSense revenue.

Google Analytics Basics

Key Metrics

  • Users: Unique visitors
  • Sessions: Total visits
  • Pageviews: Total page views
  • Bounce Rate: Percentage of single-page visits
  • Average Session Duration: Time spent on site
  • Pages/Session: Average pages viewed per visit

Traffic Sources

Organic Search

  • Google: Primary search engine traffic
  • Keywords: Search terms users use to find you
  • Landing Pages: Pages users land on from search

Referral Traffic

  • Social Media: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
  • Backlinks: Links from other websites
  • Referral Sites: Sites sending you traffic

Direct Traffic

  • Direct: Users typing URL directly
  • Bookmarks: Users with bookmarked pages

User Behavior Analysis

Content Performance

  • Top Pages: Most visited pages
  • Engagement: Time on page, bounce rate
  • Exit Pages: Pages users leave from

User Flow

  • Entry Pages: Where users enter your site
  • Navigation Path: Pages users visit in sequence
  • Goal Conversions: Actions users complete

AdSense Analytics

  • Page Views: Total ad impressions
  • Clicks: Total ad clicks
  • CTR: Click-through rate
  • CPC: Cost per click
  • RPM: Revenue per 1000 page views

Data-Driven Decisions

  • Content Strategy: Create more of what works
  • Traffic Sources: Focus on high-converting sources
  • Ad Optimization: Test ad placements and formats
  • SEO Strategy: Target high-performing keywords
